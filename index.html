About me

I am Qiong Li (李琼), a postdoctoral researcher in observational extragalactic astronomy at the University of Manchester. My research focuses on galaxy protoclusters, quasars, and large-scale structures in the early Universe using multi-wavelength facilities such as JWST, ALMA, VLA, JCMT, and Keck.

Research interests include:

  • Formation and evolution of protoclusters at high redshift
  • Quasar activity and galaxy mergers during the Epoch of Reionization
  • Multi-phase circumgalactic medium of Lyα nebulae in overdense fields
